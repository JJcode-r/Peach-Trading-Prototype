Peach Trading Floor Forex Masters Competition

Peach Trading Floor Forex Masters Challenge 2025

Powered by Peach Tech Group

Tagline: Trade. Compete. Win.

Overview:


The Forex Masters Challenge 2025 is a quarterly global trading competition hosted by Peach Trading Floor, a subsidiary of Peach Tech Group. Traders from around the world compete for cash prizes, career opportunities, and global recognition.


Competition Structure:


Phase 1: Online Qualifiers (1 Month)


Traders compete in a demo trading environment to achieve the highest percentage account growth. The Top 10 performers advance to the Grand Finale.


Phase 2: Grand Finale (2 Weeks)


Held at Peach Trading Floor HQ in Bayelsa, Nigeria, finalists will trade with live funded accounts for two weeks.


Accommodation: Provided for all finalists during the finale.


Prizes: Cash rewards range from $500 to $10,000, depending on the specific competition round.

Career Opportunity: Top 3 traders can choose to stay on as professional traders, managing funded accounts with Peach Trading Floor.


Why Participate?


FREE Enrolment: No cost to join.

Retain 100% of profits earned during the finale.

Compete for life-changing prizes and career opportunities.

Gain global exposure through YouTube documentation and Sunday Live Shows, featuring data-driven performance reviews and insights.

Frequency:


This competition takes place every 3 months, giving traders multiple opportunities to compete and excel.

Sponsorship Opportunities:
We welcome sponsors to collaborate with us in empowering the next generation of traders.


Contact Us: peachtradingfloor@gmail.com

Location:
Peach Trading Floor HQ, Bayelsa State,  and Lagos, Lekki, Nigeria
